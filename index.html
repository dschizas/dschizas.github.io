About

I am a software engineer at the intersection of product development and user experience.

I have worked on multiple levels of application stacks. For the last four years, I have been focusing on shifting traditional insurance products' direction by introducing modern technologies for their core architecture. In Azur, we use technology to change how carriers are handling customer acquisition, risk selection, underwriting and pricing.

The 2020 pandemic helped us attract talent without geographical restrictions. I feel lucky to work with engineers based in various locations (Mexico, Canada, UK, Poland, India and the Philippines) and tackle the challenge of being 100% remote with my team.

In 2012, I co-founded and developed an e-commerce travel website, captainwise.com.

These days, I am working on a new product, docex.io. docex.io aims to make the document generation process as simple as a click of a button.
